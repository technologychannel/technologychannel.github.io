About Mentor

Dhanu Gurung is a Mentor, IT Coach and an Entrepreneur.

Currently, he is working as a project lead in a startup CloudYuga Guru and helping in building a product that help learners with hands-on trainings.

He has been teaching independently from 2019 and helping IT students in gaining skills needed for software development job.

Previously, he has worked as software developer ranging from startups to MNC and has around 11 years of IT experience. He had profound knowledge on software development and went through different roles from Quality Assurance, Full Stack Web Developer, DevOps Engineer and Team Lead.

He is an Open Source Advocate and like to do community work. He is also mentoring TECH Setu which is tech community in Nepal working on bridging the gap between IT Students and IT Industry.
